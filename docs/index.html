MANGO FARM

Exporting Kesar Mangoes from Zar and around (Gir), Gujarat.

MANGO FARM is a fruit export business specializing in exporting the sweetest mangoes from Zar, a village in Gir area, Gujarat. With three generations of experience in fruit farming, we take pride in providing the best quality mangoes and canned pulp to our buyers.

Our Business

Kesar Mangoes and Canned pulp Export ..for now !! Expansion in progress !

At MANGO FARM, we export APEDA Approved quality Kesar Mangoes from the village Jhar and around that possess a G.I Tag of The Gir in Gujarat, India. We ensure that the mangoes are of the highest quality and meet all the necessary regulations. We take care of all the logistical regulations to ensure that the mangoes are delivered to you in the best condition.
