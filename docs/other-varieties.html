Other Varieties

Beyond our renowned Kesar Mangoes, we take pride in offering a diverse selection of mango varieties sourced from different corners of the country. Partnering with dedicated farmers, each of whom cherishes their produce, our collection is a testament to the rich diversity of Indian mangoes. The harvest is a result of a harmonious blend of natural cultivation techniques, ensuring that every mango we provide maintains a consistently high quality. Explore the vibrant tapestry of flavors and textures from our collaborator farmers, each fruit a celebration of the land it hails from.

01

Alphonso

GI of Maharashtra

Alphonso Mangoes, hailed as the "King of Mangoes," are a regal delight in the Indian orchards. Renowned for their exquisite flavor, aromatic fragrance, and vibrant saffron-hued pulp, Alphonso Mangoes are a symbol of premium quality.

02

Malihabadi Dashehari

GI of Uttar Pradesh

Malihabadi Dashehari Mangoes, originating from the historical city of Malihabad in India, are a testament to exquisite flavor and heritage. Celebrated for their elongated shape, bright yellow skin, and luscious, fiber-free pulp, these mangoes offer a delightful taste that balances sweetness with a hint of tanginess.

03

Bhagalpuri Zardalu

GI of Bihar

Bhagalpuri Zardalu Mangoes, hailing from the fertile orchards of Bhagalpur in Bihar, India, are prized for their distinct taste and fragrance. Revered for their large size and unique shape, Bhagalpuri Zardalus are a culinary delight, whether savored fresh or transformed into delectable mango-based dishes.

04

Laxman Bhog

GI of West Bengal

Laxman Bhog Mangoes, named after the revered deity Laxman, originate from the orchards of West Bengal, India. Characterized by their petite size, vibrant golden skin, and fragrant aroma, these mangoes boast a rich sweet taste and juicy flesh.
