From orchard block to your port

A controlled, documented process at every stage - engineered for quality, compliance and on-time delivery.

01

Sourcing & grading

Fruit is hand-picked at optimal maturity from GI-tagged Gir orchards, then graded by size, weight, brix and appearance to a consistent export specification.

02

Quality control & compliance

Residue testing, hot-water treatment and phytosanitary handling, with APEDA documentation prepared for the destination market.

03

Box packing & dispatch

Pre-cooling, box packing to market specification and controlled-atmosphere storage preserve firmness and shelf life, with consignments dispatched to schedule.

04

Logistics & documentation

Reefer container or air-freight loading with full export documentation - shipped on FOB or CIF terms with live status updates.

Lead times

Fresh fruit dispatched within days of harvest; pulp and powder available for year-round scheduling.

Payment

Advance & balance against documents or L/C - confirmed per order and market.
