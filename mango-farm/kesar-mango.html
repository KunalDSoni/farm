Kesar Mangoes

" In Zar's embrace, where sunsets paint the sky, Gir's fertile whispers, secrets from on high. Kesar mangoes, born of soil so rare, In nutrients rich, they grow with care. Each mango a gem, kissed by the sun, Gir's bounty, a tale of soil well-spun. Zar's orchards dance, a vibrant hue, Nature's masterpiece, in every view."

Take a moment to savor the beauty of Kesar mangoes, a beloved variety flourishing in the heart of Gujarat, Zar, India — specifically in the Gir region! These mangoes boast a unique charm with their sweet, fruity taste, vibrant yellow-orange flesh, and a delicately thin skin that peels away effortlessly. What sets them apart is not just their renowned aroma but also the delightful texture that distinguishes them from other mango varieties.

The magic of Kesar mangoes unfolds in the very soil they call home. Nestled in the Gir region, Zar, this locale is blessed with fertile soil, abundant sunlight, and a moderate climate — the perfect recipe for cultivating exquisite mangoes.

Kesar Mango features

01

Abundant Production

India dominates global mango production, supplying 56% of the world's mangoes. This extensive yield ensures a constant supply of Indian mangoes, catering to both domestic needs and international export demands.

02

Huge Export Market

Indian mangoes, including the prized Kesar variety, find their way to numerous countries, with the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and more standing out as notable importers. The widespread global demand is a testament to their exceptional quality and irresistible appeal.

03

5 Decade Old Trees

Older orchards have well-established trees that have been producing fruit for a longer time. This can contribute to the overall quality and flavor of the mangoes.

04

APEDA Approved Mango Farms

The Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) approval ensures that the mangoes meet export-quality standards. It's a mark of quality and adherence to regulations.

05

Guidelines of Good Agricultural Practices

Following Good Agricultural Practices (GAP) guarantees the cultivation of mangoes in an eco-friendly and sustainable manner. It signifies a dedication to producing fruit that is both safe and of premium quality.

06

Diverse Application

The adaptability of Kesar mangoes, characterized by their non-fibrous pulp and silky texture, renders them suitable for a range of uses. From enjoying the fruit as is to extracting juice and crafting various liquid-based products, their versatility enhances their appeal.

07

Natural Farming Methods

In our commitment to sustainable agriculture, we prioritize Natural Farming Practices — a methodology that minimizes the use of synthetic inputs while promoting eco-friendly and sustainable farming techniques. By avoiding excessive dependence on synthetic chemicals, our aim is to create a balanced and resilient ecosystem that enhances the overall health of the produce.

08

Chemical Free Mangoes

We prioritize the meticulous adherence to export-quality standards and employ a carbide-free production process. By eliminating carbide from our production practices, we prioritize the well-being of consumers and uphold our commitment to providing naturally ripened, safe, and high-quality fruits.
