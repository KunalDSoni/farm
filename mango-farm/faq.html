Frequently asked questions

MANGO FARM mangoes are distinguished by their premium quality, which is a result of our meticulous cultivation practices and commitment to authenticity. Most mangoes are sourced from our own orchards and we have tie-ups with local farmers where we combine our natural cultivation practices ensuring freshness and traceability.

We adhere to strict quality control measures at every stage of the export process. This includes regular inspections, adherence to international food safety standards, and the use of proper packaging to maintain freshness.

We specialize in Kesar mangoes, which are renowned for their exquisite taste and aroma. Additionally, we have tie-ups with local farmers for other varieties such as Alphonso, Chausa, Dashehari, and Bhagalpuri, allowing us to offer a diverse range of mangoes to our customers.
