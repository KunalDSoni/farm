The Backyard & Co.

Exporting Kesar Mangoes from Zar and around (Gir) , Gujarat.

The Backyard & Co. is a fruit export business specializing in exporting the sweetest mangoes from Zar, a village in Gir area, Gujarat. With three generations of experience in fruit farming, we take pride in providing the best quality mangoes and canned pulp to our buyers.
