Kesar Mangoes

" In Zar's embrace, where sunsets paint the sky,
Gir's fertile whispers, secrets from on high.

Each mango a gem, kissed by the sun,

Zar's orchards dance, a vibrant hue,
Nature's masterpiece, in every view."

Take a moment to savor the beauty of Kesar mangoes, a beloved variety flourishing in the heart of Gujarat, Zar, India. Specifically in the Gir region! These mangoes boast a unique charm with their sweet, fruity taste, vibrant yellow-orange flesh, and a delicately thin skin that peels away effortlessly. What sets them apart is not just their renowned aroma but also the delightful texture that distinguishes them from other mango varieties.

 

The magic of Kesar mangoes unfolds in the very soil they call home. Nestled in the Gir region, Zar, this locale is blessed with fertile soil, abundant sunlight, and a moderate climate – the perfect recipe for cultivating exquisite mangoes. The resulting produce is hailed globally as some of the finest, a testament to the harmonious blend of these natural blessings.

 

What makes them attractive is the meticulous cultivation process. Here, in the orchards, skilled farmers tend to the mango trees with a touch of tradition. Embracing organic fertilizers and natural pest control methods, they ensure the health and purity of the mangoes, steering clear of harmful chemicals. It's this dedication to craftsmanship that elevates our Kesar mangoes, making each bite a celebration of nature's bounty and time-honored farming practices.

Kesar Mango features

01

ABUNDANT PRODUCTION

India dominates global mango production, supplying 56% of the world's mangoes. This extensive yield ensures a constant supply of Indian mangoes, catering to both domestic needs and international export demands.

02

HUGE EXPORT MARKET

Indian mangoes, including the prized Kesar variety, find their way to numerous countries, with the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and more standing out as notable importers. The widespread global demand for Indian mangoes is a testament to their exceptional quality and irresistible appeal.

03

5 DECADE OLD TREES

Older orchards have well-established trees that have been producing fruit for a longer time. This can contribute to the overall quality and flavor of the mangoes.

04

APEDA APPROVED MANGO FARMS

The Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) approval ensures that the mangoes meet export-quality standards. It's a mark of quality and adherence to regulations.

05

GUIDELINES OF GOOD AGRICULTURAL PRACTICES

Following Good Agricultural Practices (GAP) guarantees the cultivation of mangoes in an eco-friendly and sustainable manner. It signifies a dedication to producing fruit that is both safe and of premium quality.

06

DIVERSE APPLICATION

The adaptability of Kesar mangoes, characterized by their non-fibrous pulp and silky texture, renders them suitable for a range of uses. From enjoying the fruit as is to extracting juice and crafting various liquid-based products, their versatility enhances their appeal in different culinary applications.

07

NATURAL FARMING METHODS

In our commitment to sustainable agriculture, we prioritize Natural Farming Practices, a methodology that minimizes the use of synthetic inputs while promoting eco-friendly and sustainable farming techniques. This approach goes beyond conventional farming methods, emphasizing the importance of working in harmony with nature. By avoiding excessive dependence on synthetic chemicals, our aim is to create a balanced and resilient ecosystem that enhances the overall health of the produce. This dedication to Natural Farming not only contributes to environmental conservation but also ensures that the fruits we cultivate are of the highest quality, reflecting the richness of the land and the care invested in their growth.

08

CHEMICAL FREE MANGOES

We prioritize the meticulous adherence to export-quality standards and employ a carbide-free production process. This commitment is not just about meeting international regulations; it is a dedication to delivering the highest quality and safest produce to our consumers worldwide. Carbide, a chemical used to artificially ripen fruits, can pose health risks if not handled properly. By eliminating carbide from our production practices, we prioritize the well-being of consumers and uphold our commitment to providing naturally ripened, safe, and high-quality fruits
