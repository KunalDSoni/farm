Fruitful Fusion: Embrace the Vibrancy of Mango Pulp !

Mango pulp

Introducing our luscious Mango Pulp, a pure essence of tropical bliss captured in every spoonful. Sourced from the finest, ripest mangoes, our pulp embodies the vibrant colors, rich flavors, and irresistible sweetness of the sun-kissed fruit. Each jar is a testament to quality and authenticity, crafted with care to preserve the natural essence of mangoes at their peak. Versatile and convenient, our mango pulp is perfect for adding a burst of flavor to your favorite recipes, from decadent desserts to refreshing smoothies. Elevate your culinary creations with the taste of summer, courtesy of our premium Mango Pulp.

Canning the pulp

We take pride in preserving the vibrant essence of ripe mangoes through meticulous canning, ensuring that every jar retains the natural flavors and nutrients of the fruit. Our state-of-the-art facilities and rigorous quality control measures guarantee the highest standards of hygiene and safety. With our canning service, you can enjoy the taste of premium mango pulp year-round, perfect for creating delicious treats and refreshing beverages whenever the craving strikes.

TB&C pulp

We also supply pulp in our own packaging. Our branding serves as a symbol of trust, ensuring that when you choose The Backyard & Co., you're choosing nothing but the best. With our own packaging and branding, we're able to maintain complete control over the entire process, from sourcing the finest mangoes to delivering a product that exceeds expectations. It's our way of ensuring that every spoonful of our mango pulp embodies the spirit of The Backyard & Co. - premium quality, exceptional taste
