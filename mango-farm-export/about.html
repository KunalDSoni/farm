Who we are

Rooted in Gir. Built for export.

MANGO FARM was established in 1974 in the village of Jhar, in the heart of Gir, Gujarat — a region globally recognised for the Kesar mango and its Geographical Indication (GI) tag. What began as family cultivation is today a structured export operation.

We grow and source exclusively from the Gir belt, then grade, process, pack and ship to importers, distributors, retail chains and food manufacturers worldwide — with documentation and quality control at every step.
